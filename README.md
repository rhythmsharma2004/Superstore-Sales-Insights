Superstore Sales Insights Dashboard
Tools Used

Tableau, Excel

Role

Data Analyst

Objective

Analyze the Superstore dataset to uncover trends in sales, profit, customer segments, and product categories, and build interactive dashboards to support data-driven decision-making.

Project Overview

This project analyzes the Superstore dataset, which includes sales records from multiple states, regions, categories, and customer segments.
The objective is to extract key business insights and present them through Tableau dashboards and a multi-step story.

Key Analyses Performed
1. Yearly & State-wise Sales Analysis

Visualized total sales across different states and years.

Identified top-performing regions and low-revenue areas.

2. Quarterly Sales Trends

Observed seasonal variations, including consistently lower Q1 sales and stronger Q4 performance.

Applied year-level filters to compare performance over time.

3. Category & Sub-category Performance

Compared product categories across multiple years.

Identified growth and decline trends across specific product types.

4. Sales vs. Profit Relationship

Built scatter and line charts to analyze profitability across regions and timelines.

5. Customer Segment Analysis

Examined buying behavior across customer segments.

Identified the most profitable segments to support marketing and inventory decisions.

6. Interactive Dashboards & Story

Created dashboards using maps, bar charts, line charts, and tables.

Developed a Tableau Story summarizing key insights for stakeholders.

Key Insights

Sales typically drop in Q1 and peak in Q4 across multiple years.

Some states generate high sales but low profit, indicating potential pricing or discounting issues.

Technology and Office Supplies categories show stronger margins compared to certain Furniture sub-categories.

Corporate and Home Office customer segments contribute significantly to overall profitability.
